WHO IS SHAFIQ AKBAR?

Mr. Akbar is one of the most accomplished entrepreneurs in Pakistan. He is regarded as the pioneer in revolutionizing the Real Estate sector of our country. His pursuit for sustainable transformation started 22 years back with Hyundai Engineering & Constructions where he was appointed to various mega projects including Chashma Hydro-Power Project and Pirkoh Gas Compression Power Plant. Soon after, he kicked off his profound career in real estate by becoming certified financial advisor for Douglas Allen, UK.
